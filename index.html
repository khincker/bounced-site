Instant Switching

Toggle between versions with zero gap. No loading, no buffering. Hear the transition in real time, right where you're listening.

PCM Waveforms

Real waveform rendering from the actual audio data. Not a generic thumbnail — a true visual representation of what you're hearing.

⟳

Loop Regions

Select any section and loop it. Compare the chorus. Compare the low end in the bridge. Focus on the part that matters.

Loudness Matching

Automatic level normalization. Compare the sound, not the volume. Stop being fooled by the louder version sounding "better."

△

Drift Map

See where two tracks actually differ. Bounced subtracts the raw audio sample-by-sample and maps the energy difference across the timeline. Not just volume — mix changes, different words, added instruments.

Auto Alignment

Different lead-ins, different export points, different file lengths — Bounced detects where the audio matches and syncs from there. Just upload and compare.

The Gap

DAW plugins compare mixes. Web tools share them. Nobody does both.

Plugins like ADPTR Metric AB are brilliant for A/B comparison — but they're trapped inside your DAW. Your client never hears the comparison. The decision stays one-sided.

Web platforms like Mixup and Filepass let you share and collaborate — but their "version comparison" is a toggle button. No synced waveforms. No loop regions. No shareable comparison state.

Bounced puts a real comparison tool in the browser and makes it sendable.

— DAW plugins — great comparison, can't share it
— Web platforms — shareable, but no real comparison tool
— ABmyMix — great free comparison tool, but sharing requires both users to connect Dropbox and to already have access to the same files
✓ Bounced — web-native comparison you can send as a link
